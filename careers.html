Process Engineer

Paris (FR)

Full-time

As a Process Engineer at LigthTrim Photonics, you will develop and optimize our groundbreaking fabrication processes to achieve unprecedented precision in photonic devices. You will work closely with our R&D team to scale our technology for high-volume manufacturing, ensuring zero optical loss and sub-picometer accuracy.

Responsibilities:
- Develop, characterize, and optimize photonic fabrication processes.
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to integrate new processes into production.
- Troubleshoot and resolve technical challenges to improve yield and performance.
- Design experiments and analyze data to drive process improvements.

Requirements:
- MS/PhD in Electrical Engineering, Physics, Materials Science, or related field.
- 2+ years of experience in semiconductor or photonic device fabrication.
- Hands-on experience with cleanroom processes (lithography, etch, deposition, etc.).
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
- Familiarity with optical characterization techniques is a plus.
